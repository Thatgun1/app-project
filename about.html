About Star Trekking

Star Trekking is a site for anybody interested in celestrial events in their area. Plug in your zip code, and retrieve data on meteor showers, planets in view, solar flares, and moon phases that are optimally viewable near you.

Star Trekking uses data from Google Earth and from NASA's Predict the Sky API.

This website was created by Tiffany Lewis, Christopher Brewer, Matthew Boyle, and Brian Pattee.
