What is Star Trekking?

Star Trekking will help make any casual stargazer look like an expert.

    Using Geolocation, the user is provided with a plethora of information:
  • Maps of local parks
  • Weather and cloud-cover
  • Location of all planets in our solar system
  • A star chart based on the exact longitude and latitude

    • Star Trekking uses data from Google Earth and from NASA's Predict the Sky API.
